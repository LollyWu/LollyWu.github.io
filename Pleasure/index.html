Scenario 1: Concave Up (Sagging)

Zoom: Slower then Faster (Low slope first)

Pan: Faster then Slower (High horiz. speed first)

Result: The curve stays low and moves across quickly, then shoots up.

Scenario 2: Concave Down (Arching)

Zoom: Faster then Slower (High slope first)

Pan: Slower then Faster (Low horiz. speed first)

Result: The curve shoots up along P first, then crosses over high up.

Scenario 3: Zig-Zag (Switchback)

1. P(x1) → Q(x3)

2. Minify to x1.5, pan back towards P.

3. Magnify to Q(x4).

Result: An oscillation or "Z" shape path.
